  description: Camels were introduced for construction and transport. As cameleers
    left and cars were introduced in the 1920s and 1930s, camels were released and
    became feral.
  notes: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_feral_camel

  description: The first signs of feral cats were seen around Sydney in the 1820s.

    in the Northern Territories.
  notes: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cats_in_Australia#Feral_cats

    hunting.
